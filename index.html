About Ping-Pong
Epicodus project
Week 3
See GitHub page

It takes a number and returns a range of numbers from 1 to the chosen number with the following exceptions:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "ping-pong"
